Dataset1 description

MWT1-The 1st minute Walking Test is a measure of the distance a person with COPD can walk in six minutes.

MWT2-The 2nd minute Walking Test is another measure of the distance a person with COPD can walk in six 
minutes.

FEV1-Forced Expiratory Volume in 1 second is a lung function test that measures the amount of air a person 
can exhale forcefully in one second.

FVC-Forced Vital Capacity is a lung function test that measures the amount of air a person can exhale forcefully after taking a deep breath.

CAT-COPD Assessment Test is a tool used to assess the impact of COPD on a person's life by measuring their symptoms and functional status.

HAD- Hospital Anxiety and Depression scale is a tool used to assess a person's level of anxiety and depression.

SGRQ- St. George's Respiratory Questionnaire is a tool used to assess a person's quality of life related to respiratory problems.

AGE quartiles- refer to the division of a population into four groups based on their age, with each group representing one-fourth (or 25%) of the population.
